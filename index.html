Our Professional ICT Infrastructure Deployment Process

Internetworking Solutions Ltd follows a proven and professional ICT infrastructure deployment process designed to deliver secure, scalable and high-performance technology solutions for modern organizations.

Our engineers specialize in the design, installation and maintenance of CCTV surveillance systems, enterprise Wi-Fi networks, structured data cabling infrastructure, IP PBX communication systems, cybersecurity platforms and biometric access control solutions.

Every project begins with a detailed site survey and network assessment to understand the client's operational needs, facility layout and security requirements.

We then design optimized network architecture and deploy reliable enterprise-grade equipment to ensure stable connectivity, secure monitoring and efficient digital communication.

Our structured approach helps businesses, schools, hospitals, hotels and government institutions across Kenya build reliable ICT infrastructure that improves security, productivity and long-term operational efficiency.

01

Professional Site Survey & Infrastructure Assessment

Our engineers conduct a comprehensive on-site technical survey to evaluate network coverage, security vulnerabilities and infrastructure requirements. This includes CCTV camera placement analysis, wireless coverage mapping, network rack location planning and structured cabling pathways. A detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and technical proposal are then prepared to ensure the most efficient and cost-effective solution.

02

System Design & Engineering Planning

After the site survey, our engineers develop detailed system designs including network topology diagrams, IP addressing plans, wireless heat maps and security system architecture. The design phase ensures the CCTV systems, enterprise Wi-Fi networks, access control systems and communication platforms operate reliably and integrate seamlessly with existing infrastructure.

03

Professional Installation & System Configuration

Our certified technicians deploy and configure all infrastructure components including CCTV cameras, network switches, wireless access points, IP PBX communication systems and biometric access control devices. All installations follow professional cabling standards, proper labeling, secure device configuration and thorough system testing to ensure optimal performance and long-term reliability.

04

System Handover, Training & Technical Support

After installation, we provide complete system documentation, administrator training and user guidance to ensure clients fully understand how to manage their ICT infrastructure. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), remote technical support and preventative maintenance services to guarantee continuous system uptime and long-term operational reliability.
